Local Favorites

Top 3 Local Favorites

Meskla Chamoru Fusion Bistro

Start off with the Chamoru Chesa' Platter, which is a sampler of tinala' katni, chicken kelaguen, titiyas, and hinetnon tapun. Try out the BBQ chicken, short ribs, or ribs with red rice. Fried or steamed parrot fish with red rice is a great choice as well.

Proa Restaurant

Poke is a popular appetizer. Try out the Proa Style Beggars Purse, which is sushi-grade Ahi tuna encased in a furikake crepe and wasabi soy butter sauce. Get the Big Feller Trio to get a taste of the Hibachi-style short ribs, spare ribs, and chicken with red rice. End with the popular taro cheesecake to complete the experience.

Learn More

Three Squares

For breakfast, try out the Loco Moco, which is a ground beef patty on top of a bed of steamed rice and topped with house gravy and any style egg. For lunch and dinner, start off with the local sampler for that taste of chicken kelaguen, titiyas, and tinala' katni. The Fiesta Plate is a great option to get a taste the BBQ chicken and short ribs with red rice.
